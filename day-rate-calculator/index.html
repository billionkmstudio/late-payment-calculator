🔍 Hidden Costs Freelancers Miss

  • Both sides of NI — you pay employee + effectively employer contributions
  • No sick pay — a week off costs you your full day rate × 5
  • No employer pension — most employed people get 3–5% on top from their employer
  • Unpaid admin — proposals, invoicing, chasing, onboarding new clients
  • Gaps between projects — rarely 100% booked all year long
  • Professional training — keeping skills sharp costs time and money

💡 How to Use Your Number

  • This is your floor rate — never accept less
  • Check market rates on LinkedIn, Glassdoor & recruiter listings
  • Add 15–20% as your opening ask — negotiation is expected
  • Raise your rate every 12 months — inflation erodes it silently
  • Niche specialists can charge 2–3× the generalist rate
